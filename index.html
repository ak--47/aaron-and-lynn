Aaron's Vows


Lynn,

Every step matters, because we take it together. You are the most important person in my life, and you have been since the day I met you. You are my muse, my inspiration, and my other-half. You challenge me to be the very best version of myself. You light up the entire world... not just for me, but for all who are blessed to know you.

I stand before you transformed; my capacity for kindness, humility, and integrity has ever increased simply by loving you. I am forever grateful to you for helping me evolve into someone I am proud to call, me.

I promise to cherish our love and always respect you. I promise to work for our future, and live for the present. I promise to take care of you, and I promise to care for myself. I promise to be fully engaged, and I promise to give you the attention you deserve. I promise to compromise when necessary, and work towards our mutual goals, together. I promise to shepherd your growth, as you have mine, and I promise to be the rock that you can unconditionally count on.

I promise to give you all I have to offer. I want you to know that our partnership is the most significant thing in my entire universe.

And I believe to my very core, that together, we can be a beacon of hope in the world.

I love you, my moon, completely. I always have, and I always will.



Lynn's Vows


Aaron, you are my universe.

Quite often, I am overwhelmed to tears or fall to my knees with laughter – all because of the natural power of our love. Through the full spectrum of experience, I promise to support you, and to laugh with you to the ends of the earth.

Being in love with you has allowed me to find my own truth; my own strength…and I want to share all that I am with you. I know that you will hear my soul song and play it back to me, and I promise to always jam and always dance.

I will make a cozy home for us and fill it with family and positive vibrations. I will always cook delicious food, for 2, 3, or 30. I vow to give you roots and also wings.

I will take deep breaths and patiently listen, and strive to appreciate all that you offer. I will choose you every morning and kiss you every night. I promise to love you and be present in every moment of our adventure together. I will be your hope, as you are my inspiration.

From the moment we met, I knew this was it. The care and time that we have put into our relationship since that day have validated my intuition. We have grown resonant together, learned how to show our love, and practiced forgiveness. I vow to always be your partner, and share everything honestly with you. I will never stop dedicating energy to our love.

I am so excited for each step in our future together. I feel blessed to cultivate living traditions and community with you. I know we will grow old together but always be young. I promise to be your light.

You have made my dreams come true.

You complete me, Aaron. And I WILL go anywhere with you.
